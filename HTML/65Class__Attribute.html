Taipei Medical University

Taipei Medical University (TMU) was founded on June 1, 1960 by Dr. Shui-Wang Hu, Dr. Cheng-Tien Hsu and other medical professionals and devoted educators.

It is located on Wuxing Street in eastern Taipei.

FJU

FJU is a private, co-educational university founded by the Holy See, located in New Taipei City, Taiwan..

It is the oldest Jesuit-Catholic university in the Sinophone world and is known for its strong ties with the Roman Curia.

NTU

It is a national co-educational research university located in Taipei, Taiwan.

The university was founded in 1928 by the Japanese administration during the Japanese colonial era and was then known as the Taihoku Imperial University.
